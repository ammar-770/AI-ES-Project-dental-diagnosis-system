About DentalAI

DentalAI is an advanced dental analysis system designed to assist dental professionals in diagnosing conditions quickly and accurately using the power of artificial intelligence.

Our system processes dental images and automatically detects common dental conditions, providing detailed feedback and confidence scores to help dental professionals make informed decisions.

Key Features

  • Upload and analyze dental image images with AI-based analysis.
  • Instant feedback and confidence scores for detected conditions.
  • Easy access to patient records and secure logging.
  • Seamless integration with existing dental practice systems.

Our Mission

The goal of DentalAI is to improve the diagnostic process and enhance the quality of care dental professionals can offer to their patients, reducing diagnostic errors and increasing overall efficiency in dental practices.

About Our Team

This project was created by a team of dedicated students as part of the AI And Expert Systems course at NED University of Engineering And Technology, Karachi, Pakistan. We strive to create impactful AI-driven solutions in the healthcare industry.
